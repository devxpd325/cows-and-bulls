🔒Your Privacy is Safe

Before you play, here's exactly what this game does — and doesn't — do with your device.

  • ✅Your top 5 scores are saved locally in your browser's localStorage — tiny plain text, nothing else.
  • ✅No data ever leaves your device. No server, no internet request, no account.
  • ✅No cookies. No ads. No analytics. No third-party scripts of any kind.
  • ✅Nothing runs in the background. Completely idle when you're not playing.
  • ✅Erase your scores anytime using the Clear button in the scoreboard.
In short: This is just a game. As safe as opening a text file.
Cows & Bulls
A 4-digit deduction game

Guess the secret 4-digit number. All digits are unique (no repeats).

Bull = correct digit in the correct position
Cow = correct digit in the wrong position

Example: Secret is 1234, you guess 1243
→ 2 Bulls (1 & 2 right spot)  2 Cows (3 & 4 wrong spot)

Find the number in as few guesses as possible. Good luck! 🎯
